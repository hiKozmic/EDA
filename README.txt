Our dataset is about which social media platform influences peoples online shopping the most.

1.Business Understanding:
The dataset contains information about social media platforms that have influenced online shopping behavior. This data is crucial for understanding consumer preferences and can be used to inform marketing strategies and platform selection for e-commerce businesses.

2.Domain Knowledge (Job Role)
For a data analyst or marketing specialist, this information is valuable in determining where to allocate resources for social media marketing campaigns. It helps in understanding consumer behavior and preferences across different platforms.
